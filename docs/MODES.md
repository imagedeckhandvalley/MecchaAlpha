# Game Modes — Meccha Chameleon Trainer

## Normal

Classic Seeker vs Hider. **Training focus:** eyedropper accuracy, pose selection, prep-phase route planning.

## Increasing Oni (Infection-style)

Tagged Hiders become Seekers. Player count on hunt side grows each tag.

**Training focus:**
- Faster prep routines (under 60s muscle memory)
- Secondary hide spots along prep path
- Paint palettes that work under rush

## Reverse Chicken Race

Everyone paints → disguises on pedestals → observation → identify the real player.

**Training focus:**
- Disguises that survive *close* inspection
- Avoid gimmick colors that look painted-on under spotlight
- Poses that read correctly from front *and* side angles
