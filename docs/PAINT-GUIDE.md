# Paint Guide — Meccha Chameleon Trainer

## The eyedropper rule

Always sample the surface **you will physically touch** in your pose — not a similar color three meters away. Indoor lighting gradients are brutal.

## Material sliders

| Slider | When to raise | When to lower |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Metallic** | Chrome rails, glossy tiles, metal props | Matte walls, carpet, cardboard |
| **Roughness** | Chalky plaster, fabric, worn wood | Polished floors, glass, plastic |

## Quick drills

1. **Wall patch** — sample, paint, freeze against flat paint. Goal: no edge halo at 5m.
2. **Checkerboard floor** — match two adjacent squares; crouch pose mandatory.
3. **Frame mimic** — sample frame + inner wall; pose flat like a hanging picture.
4. **Balloon curl** — high roughness, round silhouette, bright single-tone maps only.

## Common tells Seekers spot

- Limbs extending past your color patch  
- Wrong lighting temperature on skin  
- Pose height doesn't match nearby objects  
- Micro-movement during freeze phase  

## Steam Workshop

Custom maps change which drills matter. Re-run **Wall patch** and **Checkerboard** on every new stage before ranked play.
